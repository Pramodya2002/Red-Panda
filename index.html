Discover Red Pandas

Red pandas, often referred to as "firefoxes," are fascinating creatures native to the bamboo forests of the Himalayas. With their striking red fur and endearing features, they capture the hearts of nature lovers worldwide.
Learn more about these charismatic animals, their habitat, behavior, and what we can do to protect them.
